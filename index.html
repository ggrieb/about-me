} Background {

Currently an aspiring software developer training at Code Fellows, and also licensed by the WSBA to practice law in the State of Washington.

Born and raised in the Puget Sound town of Everett, Washington, located at the mouth of the Snohomish River. After graduating from Gonzaga University in 2007, I moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington for graduate school. While earning my Master of Communication I worked for a media software and services company and gained experience in digital media (CMS and content production) and project management (agile).

In 2011, I left that company to attend law school where I practiced with public defenders (criminal defense attorneys) in courtrooms of both the Republic of Ireland and the United States. I also helped artisan merchants, gallery owners, and artists of various types, with their business and contract needs by working at the Washington Lawyers for the Arts (WLA) Legal Clinic.

After graduating law school in 2014, my spouse, newborn child, and I, spent about two years living in two opposite ends of the USA before returning to the Great Pacific Northwest. In June of 2017, I formed Grieb Legal PLLC, which focuses on document review and contract drafting.
